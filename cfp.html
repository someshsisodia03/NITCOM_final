Call for Papers

International Conference on Next-Gen Intelligent Technologies in Computing and Communication (NITCOM-2025)

June 13-14, 2025 | Hybrid Mode

Technically sponsored by Springer

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSED) at Dr. B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar, invites submissions to the International Conference on Next-Gen Intelligent Technologies in Computing and Communication (NITCOM-2025). This conference aims to provide a collaborative platform for researchers, academicians, scientists, and industry professionals to share innovative contributions and advancements in cutting-edge technologies.

Conference Tracks

  • Track 1: Next Generation Technology Trends
  • Track 2: Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • Track 3: Computing
  • Track 4: Security and Privacy
  • Track 5: Communication

Submission Guidelines

  • Papers must be written in English with a maximum length of 12 pages (inclusive of references).
  • All submissions should adhere to the Springer Computer Science Conference Proceedings template.
  • Papers must be submitted via the Microsoft CMT conference management system.
  • Only original, unpublished work will be considered. Simultaneous submissions to other conferences/journals are not permitted.
  • Submissions will undergo plagiarism checks using Turnitin.

Template Link: Springer Conference Proceedings Guidelines

Publication

Accepted papers will be published in SCOPUS-indexed proceedings.
