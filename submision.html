      Guidelines:

    • All the papers submitted to the conference should be written in English with a maximum page length of 12 printed pages.
    • All submissions should strictly follow the recommended Springer Computer Science Conference Proceedings template, which can be found here.
    • All the paper submissions will be done on EasyChair conference management software.
    • The submitted paper should contain original unpublished work. Simultaneous submissions (papers already submitted to other conferences/journals) are not allowed.
    • The decision regarding the acceptance of the paper is at the discretion of the Technical Program Committee.
    • Plagiarism Check using Turnitin.
    • Selected papers will be published in SCOPUS indexed proceedings.
